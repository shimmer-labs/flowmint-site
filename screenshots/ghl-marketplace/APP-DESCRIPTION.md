# FlowMint — GHL marketplace app description

Brand voice rules applied: no em dashes, active voice, no buzzwords, no AI-corporate speak.

## Short description (for the listing card, ~120 chars)

Turn any website URL into on-brand email templates pushed straight into your GHL location. Workflow-ready in minutes.

## Full description (385 chars, meets the 300 minimum)

FlowMint generates on-brand email templates for any GHL sub-account from a website URL. Paste a URL, get a brand analysis covering voice, audience, and colors. Auto-generate full email flows: welcome series, cart recovery, win-back, more. Push them straight into your GHL location with merge fields wired up correctly. You drop them into a workflow; FlowMint does the writing.

## Alternate (longer, ~520 chars, if GHL gives you more room)

FlowMint turns any website URL into on-brand email templates that push straight into your GHL location. Paste a URL, get an instant brand analysis (voice, audience, product categories, colors). Auto-generate full email flows: welcome series, cart abandonment, win-back, re-engagement, and more. Every template uses the right GHL merge-field syntax with safe fallbacks, so blank-name sends never happen. You drop the templates into your workflow; FlowMint handles the writing.

## Notes

- Built for agencies and operators who set up email flows for clients and would rather not write every email from scratch.
- "You wire it into a workflow" framing is intentional. The GHL API does not let apps build workflows, and that human-in-the-loop seam is the product, not a workaround.
- Pricing tier on GHL is freemium with the free tier active. Real pricing decisions are TBD after beta feedback.
